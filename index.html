Where in the World...?

Welcome to Where in the World...?

Welcome! This fun little game will show you a location on a map (that you can interact with) and three street level images from Google Street View. Your challenge is to guess which image corresponds to the location marked on the map. You'll get five locations on each playthrough.

  • Easy difficulty means totally random image selection - you might see the same image more than once and that helps you narrow down the answer. There's a generous 30 second time limit and you get 10 points per correct answer.
  • Hard difficulty means you'll see three images from the same city each taken within a few hundred metres of each other. Each image might show the same road layout and/or landmarks. Look closely to get the correct answer but be quick...you've only got 15 seconds to make a guess! 10 points for a correct answer and 5 points if you're nearly correct.
